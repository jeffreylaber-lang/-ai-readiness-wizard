---
What Is This?
Before your organization builds or deploys an AI chatbot or agent, there are critical questions that need honest answers — not from a technical team, but from leadership, legal, operations, and the people accountable if something goes wrong.
This tool is a structured, plain-English self-assessment that helps any stakeholder — technical or not — understand where their organization stands today across the five key areas of AI readiness.
It is designed to surface high-level gaps only. It is not a formal audit, does not constitute legal or compliance advice, and does not certify your organization against any standard. Think of it as a structured conversation starter before you commit budget, resources, and reputation to an AI initiative.
---
What It Covers
Your readiness is assessed across 5 areas, each mapped to the ISO 42001 AI Management System Standard and the NIST AI Risk Management Framework:
#	Area	What It Assesses
1	Leadership & Governance	Executive buy-in, ownership, documented purpose, vendor assessment
2	Data Privacy & Protection	Data flows, privacy impact assessments, minimization policies
3	Security & Safety Controls	Threat assessment, human oversight, content filtering, security testing
4	Fairness, Transparency & Ethics	Bias testing, user disclosure, audit logging, AI system documentation
5	Operations & Long-Term Responsibility	Deployment controls, monitoring, incident response, decommission planning
---
What You Get
After completing 27 plain-English questions (takes about 10–15 minutes), the tool generates a personalized report including:
🎓 Letter grade — A+ through F based on your overall readiness score
📊 Readiness wellness summary — scores across 6 key dimensions
🔍 Gap analysis — critical and partial gaps identified from your answers
🗓 30-60-90 day action plan — prioritized, specific actions based on exactly where your gaps are
---
Frameworks
This tool is built on two internationally recognized AI governance frameworks:
ISO 42001 — The AI Management System Standard (Annex A controls)
NIST AI RMF — The NIST AI Risk Management Framework (Govern · Map · Measure · Manage)
All framework references are mapped under the hood. The person completing the assessment never needs to know what "GOVERN 1.1" means — the questions are written in plain English so any business stakeholder can participate meaningfully.

How to Use It
Option 1 — Use the live version
Click the link at the top of this page. No setup required.
Option 2 — Run it locally
Download `ai_readiness_wizard.html`
Open it in any modern web browser
That's it — no server, no dependencies, no install
Option 3 — Host it yourself
Upload `ai_readiness_wizard.html` to any static web host, SharePoint, or intranet. The entire tool is a single self-contained HTML file.
---
Privacy
No data is collected, stored, or transmitted. All answers run entirely in the user's browser. Nothing is sent to any server. The tool works fully offline once loaded.
